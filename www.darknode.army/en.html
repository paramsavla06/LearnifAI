Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

If you want to join the fight against enemy drones, you’ll find useful information below.
[01]
What is the Darknode Unit?
Darknode is a technology-focused unit within the 412th Nemesis Brigade. We specialize in the destruction of strike UAVs such as the Geran‑2 using interceptor drones. We are building an anti‑Shahed defense architecture and sharing this expertise with other units of the Defense Forces of Ukraine.
[02]
Who commands the unit?
The founder and commander of the unit is Senior Lieutenant Oleksandr Yarmak.
[03]
Is it possible to transfer from another unit?
Yes. Please fill out the application form for active servicemembers. Our recruiters will provide information about available positions.
[04]
Can I join if I am classified
as “limited fit for service”?
Yes. You can join support roles within the unit, including logistics, engineering, communications, and related functions.
[05]
How can I support the unit?
You can support Darknode in two ways: by purchasing official unit merch or by making a charitable donation through the unit’s verified charitable foundation “Helper.” 100% of all contributions are directed toward enhancing combat capabilities and providing logistical support for our crews.
[06]
How to contact regarding partnerships or cooperation?
If you represent a business, civil organization, or government body and have partnership proposals, please contact us at: darknodemedia@gmail.com
